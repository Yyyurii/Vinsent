ABOUT THE WINE
A premium Chianti Classico Riserva made from selected vines planted at 500m next to our hilltop near Panzano, Tuscany. This wine is made from selected Sangiovese vines planted between 1989 and 1992, managed by guyot.
Sangiovese Red Sustainably Made 2018 Vecchie Terre Di Montefili Italy
WINEMAKER NOTES
by Serena Gusmeri
The Chianti Classico Riserva is made from selected Sangiovese vines planted between 1989 and 1992, managed by guyot. There is a generous spicy note to the fruit that comes through from this plot of land, with great potential for integrated tannins. To enhance and enrich the natural structure for the 2018 vintage, we experimented with the refinement, moving between barrels of 30hl and 20hl. The aging in wood will continue for around 22 months followed by at least 6 in the bottle to give that extra depth of flavour.
